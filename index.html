From the Beautiful Brain of Lwanga

Some original poem because why not

Unknown Forgotten date

Roses are red, Violets are blue, why use your head, when bootstrap will do

Okay that one was silly but this is where shit gets real. In case you didn't notice you crossed the line right up there

Wishing upon a tyrannical moon

Cue the god-hand,

a composition of those I love, respect and those whose trust I fear to lose.
It stands in support of all of my actions as if ridiculing how one might expect it to compose of naysayers.
It has formed my very being, told me what to like, what to hate, molded my vices and cast my quirks
and its divination of the brilliant future it has for me it obscures my vision for any future of my own I hold.
At centre stage...Me, subjugated to it's will, too afraid to move out from under the shadow,
so i stay, allow it's mastery of puppetry to control me, yet at every moment i plot my escape.
My wishes, my desires, my emotion buried unseen, unheard and unfelt in a place I have no memory of.
To seek aid is out of the question for my cynicism can't allow it


That one had you shook huh, so how about something a little more light then

My heart's sycophantic brain

  • My pysche pummeled at every mention
  • My life an insipid haiku to strike rhetoric
  • If to follow the code was manuduction from avarice
  • Then why, from the ether above through the heathens below, are you the fiction in my dreams?
  • Funny, the psychedelics of the human experience

I know you're wondering what's with the list? Well that is just to show that i have the understanding to create one because where else would it feature in this masterpiece
